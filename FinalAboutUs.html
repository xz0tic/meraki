Our Mission

Our mission at Meraki is to provide artists with the tools, resources and platform

for their creativity to flow freely. We are committed to building a professional

space where artwork of all mediums can be displayed by artists everywhere

and shared with the world. It's clear that technology and social media

dominate our everyday lives and we saw the need for artists to have

their own space, free from regular social media users, to display their

work. Meraki gives artists the space to expand their portfolios,

and connect with other artists, potential buyers or gallaries.

Thank you for supporting Meraki and our mission. We hope you enjoy

using Meraki as much as we did creating it.
